Welcome to Hook Head Lighthouse

Discover a Historic Gem in County Waterford

Climb to the Top of the Lighthouse

Experience breathtaking views of the Irish Sea from the top of Hook Head Lighthouse. The climb to the top is a journey through history, with the lighthouse dating back over 800 years.

Once at the top, you’ll be able to take in panoramic views of the surrounding coastline, making it the perfect spot for photography and sightseeing.

Learn About the Lighthouse's Rich History

Hook Head Lighthouse is one of the oldest operational lighthouses in the world, with a history dating back to the 12th century. The stories and legends surrounding this iconic landmark are fascinating to learn about during your visit.

Take a guided tour to hear more about the fascinating history of the lighthouse, from its construction to its role in keeping sailors safe through the centuries.

Enjoy the Beautiful Surrounding Nature

The lighthouse is located within the stunning Hook Peninsula, an area known for its natural beauty. Whether you're walking along the cliffs, exploring nearby beaches, or enjoying the local wildlife, there’s always something to see.

For nature lovers, the surrounding area offers many trails, including those that give visitors a chance to spot seals, seabirds, and other marine life along the coast.
